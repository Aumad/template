Executive Summary

This report was written to analyze a social artifact and tie it to a social issue. The social artifact I chose was the Jersey Shore. The show is tied to the social issue of our ever-deteriorating culture. The Jersey Shore encourages deviant behaviors such as drinking, pre-marital sex, and fighting. The adulterated material broad casted on this show sends a bad message to the youth of our country, encouraging behaviors against our traditional values. Proposed solutions to revive our old American culture were to; have parents more involved in children’s education so the parents’ values may have more of an influence on a child’s future actions. Another solution was to encourage the family dinner. When families spend more time together they form a bond of respect between parent and child which will lead to the passing on of values discouraging deviant behaviors.
